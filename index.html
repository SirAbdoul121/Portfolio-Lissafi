Professional Biography

COMRADE MUHAMMAD ABDURRAHMAN is a visionary entrepreneur, business strategist, and the driving force behind LISSAFI BUSINESS SOLUTIONS. Under his guidance, LISSAFI BUSINESS SOLUTIONS has grown from a startup venture into a multi-paced industry leader, serving a diverse portfolio of clients ranging from small businesses to large-scale mining operations.
